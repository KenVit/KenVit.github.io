Drink Dispenser

The purpose of our Project OneTapShots is to demonstrate dispensing a set amount of liquids through a pump from a reservoir and record what was dispensed, amount dispensed, and display to the user  what is beings dispensed along with what was previously dispensed.

This project can also be a DIY and customized for various other purposes. Such as dispensing medicine, or dispensing chemicals for other various jobs like cleaning.
